1.To find sum of n  numbers using while loop
Program:
n=int(input("Enter range:"))
sum=0
i=1
while(i<=n):
    sum=sum+i
    i+=1
    print(sum)
print("Sum of",n,"numbers:",sum)

2.To find whether a number is prime or not

num=int(input("Enter a number"))
if num>1:
    for i in range(2,num):
        if(num%i==0):
            print(num,"is not  prime")
            break
    else:
            print(num,"is prime")
else:
    print(num,"is not prime")
